Our Story

House of R is a quiet luxury fashion brand rooted in bespoke tailoring, timeless design, and Afro-futurist craftsmanship. Founded in 2014, every piece is engineered for elegance and individuality—especially for those who seek fit, function, and power in the details.

Philosophy

We believe that luxury is not loud. It’s precise. It’s personal. It’s yours. From our reversible jackets to algorithm-matched high-waist trousers, House of R builds your wardrobe around your shape, not the other way around.
